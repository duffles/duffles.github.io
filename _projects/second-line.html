--- layout: project title: Second Line Brass Band slug: second-line when: summer 2010 - summer 2015 feature: - vid: img: feature-second-line.jpg links: - site: http:secondlinebrassband.org email: mailto:secondlinebrassband@gmail.com youtube: http://www.youtube.com/user/secondlinebrassband facebook: http://www.facebook.com/slsaps category: music --- The Second Line Social Aid and Pleasure Society Brass Band is a New Orleans-style street band, based in Somerville and Cambridge, Massachusetts. We combine music with social action, slamming out the sounds of the legendary Crescent City for peace rallies, street festivals, parades, and benefits. We are also a part of the organizing force behind HONK!, a festival of activist street bands that takes place every October in Somerville’s Davis Square.
