Ghost Town is a 12 piece brass band based in Somerville, MA. We love reinventing beloved songs by arranging them for brass and play tunes ranging from pop, Brazilian, jazz, and soul to reggae, funk, disco, hip-hop, and bhangra. That said, our primary goal is just to get you dancing.
